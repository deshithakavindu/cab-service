System Usage Guidelines

Welcome to the Booking Management System

This guide will help you navigate and use our cab booking system effectively. Follow these steps to get started and make the most of our services.

Getting Started

Step 1: Log In

Visit the login page (login.html) and enter your username and password. If you don’t have an account, contact the administrator to create one.

Step 2: Access the System

After logging in, you’ll be directed to the main pages such as the Booking System, Profile, or Booking Management (for admins). Ensure you have your customer ID stored in sessionStorage to maintain access.

Step 3: Book a Cab

Navigate to the Booking Your Cab page (Booking.html). Follow these actions:

  • Select a car from the dropdown menu.
  • Choose a start date and time using the datetime picker.
  • Enter your start and stop locations to calculate the distance automatically via the map.
  • Review the calculated kilometers, tax, total amount, discount (if applicable), and final amount.
  • Submit the form to create your booking.
Step 4: Manage Bookings

Use the Profile page to view your booking history, check statuses (Upcoming, Today, Completed), and view invoices for each booking. Admins can use the Booking Management page to view, update, or delete bookings.

Tips for Effective Use

Tip 1: Use Accurate Locations

Enter precise start and stop locations in the booking form to ensure accurate distance calculations. Use the Google Maps integration for autocomplete suggestions.

Tip 2: Check Booking Dates

Ensure the start date and time are set for today or in the future. The system prevents bookings in the past for accuracy.

Tip 3: Review Charges

Before submitting a booking, review the calculated charges, including the rate per kilometer (Rs. 150), tax (10%), and any applicable discount (10% for distances over 100 km).

Tip 4: Save Your Invoice

After creating a booking, view and download your invoice from the Invoice page. Use the print or PDF download options for records.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues, try the following:

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
  • Clear your browser cache or try a different browser.
  • Contact the admin at admin@gmail.com for support.
